Confirm exact dates and Canvas assignment groups with the course coordinator
before publishing. The weights below follow the established course pattern from
the previous assignment structure.

|---|---|---|---|
| Philosophy assignment | on paper | 2026-09-16 in class | 15% |
| Literature seminars | none / attendance | completion recorded after 2026-09-29 | 15% |
| Proposal | online PDF upload | 2026-10-09, 23:59 | part of proposal 15% |

| Proposal presentation | online slide upload | evening before 2026-10-13 | part of proposal 15% |

## Notes From Previous Course

The HT2025 course used separate assignments for the scientific proposal and the
popular science abstract. It also used EasyChair for first reports and reviews,
with Studium assignment pages containing the instructions.

For HT2026, keep the same research-cycle structure. Add local-model requirements
inside the proposal, reports, reviews, and presentation instructions.
