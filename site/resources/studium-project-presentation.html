Useful Local Models

Studium Assignment Text: Project Presentation

You will present your final project at the course workshop. Attendance at the

full workshop is obligatory.

Prepare slides and submit them here by the evening before the workshop.

Presentation timing:

  • 12 minutes presentation;
  • a few minutes for questions;
  • timing will be strict.

Your presentation should include:

  • motivation and research question;
  • data and baseline;
  • model/training method;
  • main evaluation result;
  • one concrete example, demo screenshot, or local run transcript;
  • most important limitation;
  • what changed after peer review.

For Useful Local Models, make the local artifact visible. You do not need to run

a live demo, but the audience should understand what another person can run or

inspect after the course.

If you miss the workshop, contact the course coordinator and your supervisor as

soon as possible.
