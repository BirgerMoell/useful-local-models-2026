# Studium Assignment Text: Popular Science Abstract

Submit the popular science abstract for your project here.

The abstract should explain your project to a general reader who is not familiar
with language technology, machine learning, or your specific research area.

The abstract must:

- describe what problem the project studies;
- explain why the problem matters;
- explain what you will do in plain language;
- avoid unexplained technical terms;
- stay within 2000 characters including spaces.

Submit the abstract as a plain `.txt` file without formatting.

The length limit is strict. The abstract should not be longer than 2000
characters and should not be much shorter.
