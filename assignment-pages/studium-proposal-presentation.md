# Studium Assignment Text: Proposal Presentation

You will present your project proposal orally in your theme group.

Prepare slides and submit them here by the evening before the presentation. For
campus seminars, this helps everyone present from the teacher computer and avoids
technical delays.

Suggested presentation length: around 8 minutes, followed by questions and
discussion.

Your presentation should cover:

- research question;
- local use case;
- data source and split plan;
- baseline;
- model and training plan;
- evaluation plan;
- local artifact plan;
- main risk or fallback option.

This is mainly a feasibility and feedback session. You should be ready to start
working after the proposal stage, but the proposal is not a contract. If the
project changes substantially later, discuss the change with your supervisor.

If you miss the presentation seminar, contact your supervisor to arrange a later
oral presentation.
