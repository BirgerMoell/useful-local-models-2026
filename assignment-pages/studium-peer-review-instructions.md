# Studium Assignment Text: Peer Review

Your task is to review two papers written by your peers. Write a textual review
of each paper. Do not assign numerical scores unless the course coordinator
explicitly asks for them.

Review both the scientific paper and the reproducibility of the model claims.

Do not run unsafe code. You are not required to execute the model. You may inspect
the paper, README, model card, dataset card, configs, and reported outputs.

Treat the papers confidentially. Do not share them with other people or upload
them to external AI tools or other software services.

This restriction is about confidential peer work. It does not change the general
theme policy that AI tools may be used responsibly on your own code,
experiments, and public materials when disclosed.

In your review, comment on:

- whether the research question is clear;
- whether the baseline is meaningful;
- whether the data is sufficiently documented;
- whether the training method is reproducible;
- whether the evaluation supports the claims;
- whether the local runnable artifact is described clearly;
- whether ethical considerations are concrete;
- what should be improved before the final version.

Useful review structure:

- short summary of the paper;
- strengths;
- major issues;
- minor issues;
- questions for the author;
- concrete suggestions for the final version.

The review should be written independently in your own words and without help
from AI tools unless the course coordinator explicitly changes this policy.
