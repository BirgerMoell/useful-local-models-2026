# Studium Assignment Text: Peer Review

When reviewing a project in this theme, evaluate both the scientific paper and
the reproducibility of the model claims.

Do not run unsafe code. You are not required to execute the model. You may inspect
the paper, README, model card, dataset card, configs, and reported outputs.

In your review, comment on:

- whether the research question is clear;
- whether the baseline is meaningful;
- whether the data is sufficiently documented;
- whether the training method is reproducible;
- whether the evaluation supports the claims;
- whether the local runnable artifact is described clearly;
- whether ethical considerations are concrete;
- what should be improved before the final version.

Do not upload your peer's paper or project files to external AI tools.
