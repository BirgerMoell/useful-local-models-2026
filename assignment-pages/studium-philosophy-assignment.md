# Studium Assignment Text: Philosophy Assignment

The philosophy of science assignment is examined in class.

You will answer questions on paper during the classroom session. No books,
notes, computers, phones, or other aids are permitted. Bring your own pen; paper
will be provided.

To prepare, read Okasha, *Philosophy of Science: A Very Short Introduction*.
Either the first or second edition is fine.

Reading guidelines:

- Chapter 1: What is science?
- Chapter 2: Scientific reasoning, including induction, deduction, and inference to the best explanation.
- Chapter 3: Explanation in science.
- Chapter 5: Scientific change and scientific revolutions.

This assignment is handled by the course-responsible teacher.
